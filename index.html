About the visualizer

Some people may wonder what happens to the USA international trade. They can easily get the data from some government websites but they never have a good way to explore the data. Boring numbers can't give us a intuitive way to look at them. This is why we create the visualizer. By such an interactive tool, people may have a direct impression of the data.
